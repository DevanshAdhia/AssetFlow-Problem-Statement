# Department Head Workflow

The Department Head manages the resources and assets allocated specifically to their organizational unit.

## Responsibilities & Daily Tasks

1. **Department Visibility**
   - View and track all assets currently allocated to their specific department.
2. **Department Needs**
   - Book shared resources (e.g., conference rooms) on behalf of their entire department using the calendar view.
3. **Internal Approvals**
   - Review and approve transfer or allocation requests that originate from their department.
   - Approve transfer requests for assets targeting their department.
